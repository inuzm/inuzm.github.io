About Me

Hello! I am Imanol :)
I am currently a last year Ph.D. student at CIMAT, A.C. working in Probability under the supervision of José Luis Pérez and Adrián González Casanova. My research focuses on the investigation of exchangeable processes, and of the genealogical structure that emerges in multi-type branching processes.
